Flatten to Ground

  • Flattens the selected faces and edges to Z=0 of the current drawing axes

Tool Operation

  1. (Optional) Preselect faces and edges to move.
  2. Click a face or an edge to move all vertices to the ground (Z = 0).

Tips

  • The edges will be moved along the blue axis to Z = 0 based on the current sketch axes position and orientation.
  • If you want to move the edges to a different ground plane, first move the axes using the SketchUp Axes tool.
  • Press ESC to clear the selection.
