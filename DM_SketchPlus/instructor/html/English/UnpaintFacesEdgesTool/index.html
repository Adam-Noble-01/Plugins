Unpaint Faces and Edges

  • Recursively removes materials from only Faces and Edges within the selected entities

Tool Operation

  1. (Optional) Preselect entities to remove materials from.
  2. Click an entity and all of the materials applied to the faces and edges will be removed from it as well as from all nested faces and edges within.

Tips

  • Materials applied to groups and components will remain.
  • Remember that edges and faces with no material applied will inherit the material of the object that contains them.
  • Press ESC to clear the selection.
