Polygon Lasso

  • Select entities within a polygon by clicking points

Tool Operation

  1. Click points to create a polygon around the entities you wish to select.
  2. Click near the first point of the polygon to perform the selection or press Enter.

Modifier Keys

  • CTRL = Add to current selection
  • CTRL + SHIFT = Subtract from current selection
  • SHIFT = Toggle selection
