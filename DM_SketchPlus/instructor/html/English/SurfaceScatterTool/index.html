Sprinkle on Surface

  • Scatter objects over the pre-selected surface

Tool Operation

  1. Preselect a surface or collection of faces.
  2. Activate the tool.
  3. Tap Up or Down arrow to change density.
  4. Click a group or component to sprinkle over the selected surface(s).
  5. (Optional) Continue clicking objects to sprinkle more instances on the same surface.

Modifier Keys

  • CTRL = Toggle align to surface or global up axis.

Tips

  • Key-in a number between 1 and 100 to change the sprinkle density.
