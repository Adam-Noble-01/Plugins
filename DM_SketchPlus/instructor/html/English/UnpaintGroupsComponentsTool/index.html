Unpaint Groups and Components

  • Recursively removes materials from only Groups and Components within the selected entities

Tool Operation

  1. (Optional) Preselect groups and components to remove materials from.
  2. Click a group or component and its material will be removed as well as from all nested groups and components within.

Tips

  • Materials applied to edges and faces will remain.
  • Remember that edges and faces with no material applied will inherit the material of the object that contains them.
  • Press ESC to clear the selection.
