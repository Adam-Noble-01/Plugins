Change Axes+

  • Change the Axes location for the selected group or component to one of 27 preset positions

Tool Operation

  1. (Optional) Preselect a single group or component.
  2. Click a group or component.
  3. Click one of the axes location grips to move the object's axes to the new position.

Modifier Keys

  • CTRL = Show bottom center grip only

Tips

  • Press ESC to select a different object.
