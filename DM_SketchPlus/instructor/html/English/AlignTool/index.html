Align

  • Align the selected objects with each other

Tool Operation

  1. Preselect two or more groups or components to align.
  2. Click a grip on the alignment box to set the plane and align the selected objects to that plane.

Modifier Keys

  • CTRL = Align center of objects instead of max extent of objects

Tips

  • Press ESC to reset and re-orientate the alignment box to the current drawing axes.
